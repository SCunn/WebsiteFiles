TRACES PHOTOGRAPHY PROJECT

The Traces photograpghy project theme was based on movement and time.  the majoity of my Images  were taken on a Nikon D3200 DSLR camera.  My Images are minutley edited on Photoshop as i believe that to much editing can ruin the natural elements of the image, i would prefer to stay true to what was caught in the moment.  some of these images were also shot on a HTC mobile phone where as others were taken using a macro lense for extreme close up shots.
