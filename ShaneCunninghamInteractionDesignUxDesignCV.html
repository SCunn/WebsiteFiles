Profile 

Interactive media graduate with an adept knowledge in user experience design skills and user centered methodologies gained through the completion of individual and collaborative projects.  During the economic downturn I took the opportunity to re-train and explore the prospect of a career in a more creative field.  From my time at the Limerick School of Art and Design I excelled in all aspects and gained a highly creative perspective with a focus on thinking outside the box.  My passion for technology and people’s relationship with technology led to further study in the area of Interaction Design whereby I completed a Masters degree in Interactive Media at the University of Limerick.  I am a driven, hard-working, highly adaptable individual who is competent in all aspects of the design process.
