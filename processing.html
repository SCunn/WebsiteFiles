Creative Coding

The video above is the result of a Processing sketch I was working on where I was exploring how to track motion through the computers webcam as a means of control for a audio synthesizer constructed in Pure Data.  The Processing sketch works by taking a second screenshot and canceling out the colours then returns the  average distance  between two colours when movement is detected.  the sketch also uses the oscp5 library to send messages to netreceive in Pure Data.
